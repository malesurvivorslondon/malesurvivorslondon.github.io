Services for Male Services

Below is a list of groups who have said they provide services to male survivors. We aware that with the continued cut backs in spending this list may quickly be out of date, so apologies in advance if that is the case and let us know if you can so we can delete it. Also if anything new starts up please let us know so we can add it to the list. It is clear that still despite all of the media focus on abuse issues services are extremely limited. In London there is next to nothing and most men are still reliant on the willingness of a GP to go into bat for them or mental health services when a crisis occurs, this is unacceptable and part of our job is to make people aware of the need for change.
